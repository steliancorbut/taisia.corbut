  Bio

The translation of emotional states into visual metaphors that particularly capture the everyday man’s attempt to face uncertainty represents a point o congruence of personal research in recent years. Oscillating between drawing, deep relief and object, I seek to transpose the fragility of the psyche in the face of the unknown, from a personal perspective. The dialogue created between the sensitive lines or elements and the harshness of the others underline the ambivalence of the being and it’s ability to move from one pose to another.
