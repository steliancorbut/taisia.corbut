  Bio

Taisia Corbut (b.1997, Romania) is a visual artist whose practice unfolds at the intersection of visual poetry, material exploration, and cultural memory. A graduate of the University of Art and Design in Cluj-Napoca, with a study residency at Birmingham City University, Taisia works across installation, sculpture, drawing, and experimental media. Her works often emerge through layered gestures and conceptual precision, reflecting on identity, language, and the quiet politics of transformation.
