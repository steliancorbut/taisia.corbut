My name is ophelia

My name is ophelia
and I am rain, dust and promises
I am a bare soul
that collapsed and rebuilt itself 1000 times before
They left, so I became ashes
That fell on a field
So, I became flowers and rivers
and then, when they came back, they built upon my soul, a place for them to live forever
So first, I turned into plaster
Then nails filled my mouth
until I became iron.
Now, I can be reshaped, transformed
But I will not break as easy as I did before.
My name is Ophelia
And I became a principle of order out of chaos.
