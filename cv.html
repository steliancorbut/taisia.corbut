  Oscillating between drawing, deep relief and object, I seek to transpose the fragility of the psyche in the face of the unknown, from a personal perspective.

About Me

The translation of emotional states into visual metaphors that particularly capture the everyday man’s attempt to face uncertainty represents a point of congruence of personal research in recent years.
